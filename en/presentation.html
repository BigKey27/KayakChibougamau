Tourisme Chibougamau

presents the most populous city of the Eeyou Istchee Baie-James region, CHIBOUGAMAU.

Our mission is to promote our city by presenting its attractions and the people who live there.

Discover the heart of Northern Quebec by browsing its history and these warm inhabitants.

Nestled in the heart of the wild forest, Chibougamau, outdoor life and adventure. We could also say mines and forests. These are the main characteristics of our economy and this is what gave birth to our city around 1956.

Chibougamau means 'meeting place', what a good excuse to come and meet us and live this unique experience with us.

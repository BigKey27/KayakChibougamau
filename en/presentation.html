Plein Air Ungava - Kayak

Is a group of amateur kayak enthousiasts from Chibougamau/Chapais.

Our mission is to share information about access to the different lakes so you can practice your favourite sport safely.

You can find a few alternate paths labelled (A-B) to try out on the interactive map.

All outlined paths are suitable for all experience levels.

Looking forward to seeing you on the water!

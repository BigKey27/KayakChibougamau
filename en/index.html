Reserve Assinica (Photo: Reserve's reception)

A gem in nature

Chibougamau is surrounded by 3 wildlife reserves. Located north of Chibougamau and the native village of Oujé-Bougoumou, the Assinica wildlife reserve is popular for its fishing practice.

Covering an area of 8 885 km2, chances are you can get some nice fishing trophies.

Come and discover what characterizes our lakes and you will be able to decorate your meals with wild fish that you will have caught yourself!
